ARTIST BIO

Sammy Mutinda is a contemporary visual artist known for his expressive abstract works. Inspired by nature and human behaviours, he explores woodcut block printing, sculpture, and other disciplines to create immersive visual experiences. His work pushes the boundaries of abstraction, playing with colour, form, and emotion to invite deeper engagement. By blending traditional techniques with modern narratives, Sammy crafts dynamic compositions that challenge perception. His work has been showcased in both local and international exhibitions, earning recognition for its depth and innovation. Through his evolving practice, he continues to explore new ways of storytelling through material and medium.

ARTIST STATEMENT

I am a visual creator with the ability to transformation any material at my disposal from one form to another to fit my ideology of the world I live in. I don’t limit myself to a style or medium I give focus to the inspirations that flock in within me developing concepts that are triggered by my own interaction with the surroundings I find more strength in reduction process sculpting and woodcut print making as it gives me an intriguing way of manipulating my figures subjects. I use the indigenous way of print making as I use manual way of pressing as it give me more connection to my work. My subjects are human forms with no gender attached to them because I believe we all beings under the sun.
